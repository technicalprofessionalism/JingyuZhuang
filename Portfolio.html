Flappy Ball

  • 2017.11 - 2017.12

  • Genre: Experimental Game / Infinite Runner controlled by photocensor

  • Role: Sole Designer / Developer

  • Arduino & Processing & Super Collider

  • Player controls the little ball by moving their hands on/off the photocensor! Arduino will collect the input from the circuit and processing will take the input and give players the visual feedback. Super Collider outputs some music indicating whether the player has successfully passed a obstacle.
